YouPlea: Pretrial Diversion

YouPlea is a compact pretrial diversion portal used by citizens and prosecutors to reduce time spent in court.

Summary

I am interning at a startup, Quantworks, that builds analytics-driven solutions for a early to senior-stage companies. Since I'm interested in legal tech, I was lucky to intern as a developer for YouPlea, a partnership between North Carolina District Attorneys and Quantworks. YouPlea is an early-stage company that aims to drastically reduces court time by making components of the plea agreement and pretrial diversion processes accessible to all.

During my summer-long internship, I kickstarted early backend development on YouPlea. I worked primarily with Amazon Web Services (AWS) tools for backend functionality, user validation, and data storage. I wrote endpoints for use by both citizens and attorneys, getting to actively participate in development work at a startup. The challenge and reward of working for a smaller team is getting to wear multiple hats, and I thoroughly enjoyed interacting with frontend, backend, product, business, and sales components of the product to see how each part synthesized to create a coherent product. My backend team is pivotal in working towards a MVP as YouPlea gets off the ground running.
