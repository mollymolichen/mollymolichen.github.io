MemeMatch: Dating with Memes

MemeMatch is a fun app I came up with for my databases class, COMPSCI 316. I worked in a team of four to come up with a database-driven web application of our choice. Since meme culture has recently sprung up amongst college students throughout the nation, we decided to incorporate meme images as the main source of data for our app. Furthermore, the growing popularity of dating apps amongst people our age inspired the idea to combine memes and dating apps. We based our interface off of Tinder, a popular dating app available for iOS and Android smartphones.

Dating with memes—matching users based on similar meme preferences

Our MemeMatch app is potentially the first of its kind to match users based on common meme interests. We modified Tinder’s interface of ‘swiping right’ on people you are interested in and left on people you are not to fit meme images rather than user profiles. MemeMatch has a like and dislike option for each meme, a simple image-focused design that centers the user’s attention on one image at a time, and a matching algorithm to generate meaningful results from user input.
